Whats is SCSA?

SCSA is a novel algorithm for signal representation developed by professor Taous-Meriem Laleg-Kirati which is based on the discrete spectrum of a Schrodinger operator to analize signals as follows

Basic principle

Lets consider a signal represented by a real-valued function \(y(t)\), \(t \in \mathbb{R}\) such that for every t the function stays positive then we can define the Scrhodinger operator \(\mathcal{H}_h(y(t))\) as $$ \mathcal{H}_h(y(t))\Psi(t) = -h^2\frac{d^2}{dt^2}\Psi(t) - y(t)\Psi(t) \quad, \quad \Psi(t) \in H^2(\mathbb(R))$$

When $a \ne 0$, there are two solutions to $ax^2 + bx + c = 0$ and they are \(The following\) $$x = {-b \pm \sqrt{b^2-4ac} \over 2a}.$$
